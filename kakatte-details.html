What is Kakatte Koi Yo?

Glad you asked! Kakatte koi yo is a platform fighter game for 1-4 players. Play in 3 different modes as you battle with fish, explode bombs, and collect buffs to knock the other team out of the ring. Train in single player, fight in a free-for-all, or form teams of 1v1 or 2v2. Kakatte has been showcased at many events including E3 where it had its own booth! Find it on steam here.

What I Contributed

I worked on this game as the Lead Programmer. I created and coded 90% of the 105 scripts included in the game. My work included:

  • All gameplay modes except single player
  • All mechanics including combat, fishing, powerups, and health
  • Implementation of animations from sprite sheets
  • 3 different controller types and 2 different keyboard presets for playing that allowed icons to change based on was being used
